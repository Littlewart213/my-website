Profile

  • Kirby is a pink, short and fluffy character which can suck up objects to gain a special trait depending on the character that has been sucked. Despite his power, he is known for being very cute and small.

Fun facts:

  • Kirby is 20cm tall
  • Kirby is actually a male
  • The name 'Kirby' originated from the lawyer 'John Kirby' who helped defend Nintendo against a lawsuit
